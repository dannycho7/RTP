In RTP
1) ./make_all.sh to compile all needed source file

2) ./initial.sh <path_to_xml_data> <choice_of_representative>, to index the data, 
Possible choices for for <choice_of_representative>:
1 : for latest representative
2: for longest representative

e.g. ./initial.sh ./data/all13v.xml 1;


3)put your queries in queries_all.txt & python batch_optionTestCluster_vector_new.py to query.
